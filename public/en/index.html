Organiko is a project created to facilitate contact between small organic producers and consumers.

We deliver baskets with ecological products in Castelldefels, Gavà, Begues and neighboring towns. Our vegetables, fruits and other products are of the season and locally harvested, from the Montmany family farm and their exchanges with other organic farmers.

A growing project with all of our heart, to provide our two cents to the care of our mother Earth.

How does it work?

You can check the available products, and make your first order, in the Orders section of this site.

From then on, every Friday you'll receive an email in which we send a form with the seasonal produce for that week. You then choose your products from that simple form. You don't need to order every week, and there's no minimum quantity to order.

You can send your order before the following Monday at 10pm, indicating your full name, address, contact number and prefered delivery time. We make the deliveries every Thursday from 13:00 to 21:00.​ To the order amount we'll add 5€ for order preparation and delivery.

If you need more information, or have something to tell us, please contact us!
